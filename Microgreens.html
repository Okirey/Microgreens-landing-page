Microgreens

Adding a small amount of Microgreens to salads, dips or smoothies can instantly boost the nutritional value.

What are the health benefits of microgreens?

  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Antioxidants

Growing Microgreens is a great gardening project for beginners and it's fun to watch them peek up from the soil. Kids will enjoy helping and watching them grow, too. Microgreens are easy to grow and take up a little space, such as a window sill, and you can just simply snip off what you want to use and have it fresh right from your own garden.
